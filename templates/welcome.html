Hello!

This tool creates a playlist by detecting Spotify links in your submitted text file.

Because this will create things against your Spotify account, some permissions are required:

  • "View your Spotify account data: Your name and username, your profile picture, how many followers you have on Spotify and your public playlists" - I believe any app linking to a Spotify account requires this.
  • "Take actions in Spotify on your behalf: Create, edit, and follow private playlists & Create, edit, and follow playlists" - this tool requests the playlist-modify-private and playlist-modify-public scopes in order to create and add tracks to public and private playlists on your account.

What does this do, exactly?

You provide some parameters to create a playlist with, e.g. a name and description. Along with this, you upload a text file. The tool will detect Spotify track links within the text and create a playlist with those tracks in.

It does not look for anything else within your submitted text file, or use any other Spotify endpoints beyond those required to complete this simple task.
